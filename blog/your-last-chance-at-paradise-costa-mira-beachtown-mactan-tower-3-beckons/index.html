Your Last Chance at Paradise? Costa Mira Beachtown Mactan – Tower 3 Beckons!

Aug 26, 2025

Mactan, Cebu is fast becoming the go-to destination for those seeking the perfect blend of island tranquility and modern convenience. At the heart of this transformation is Costa Mira Beachtown, a visionary development that promises resort-style living with direct access to Mactan's stunning coastline. If you've been dreaming of a beachfront home, the time to act is now!

We've shared the exciting progress of this master-planned community before, and the momentum is only growing.


Towers 1 & 2: A Dream Already Rising

The initial phases of Costa Mira Beachtown have seen tremendous success. Towers 1 and 2 are well under construction, with structures visibly changing the Mactan skyline. These towers are a testament to the developers' commitment and the project's rapid progress. With many units in these initial towers already sold and progressing towards completion, the opportunity to own a piece of this paradise is becoming more exclusive.

But don't despair if you missed out on the first two! The good news is that your chance for an unparalleled beachfront lifestyle is still very much alive.


Your Final Opportunity: Tower 3 Now Open for Priority!

Due to overwhelming demand and the rapid success of Towers 1 and 2, Costa Mira Beachtown has swiftly launched Tower 3. This is your prime opportunity to secure a unit in this highly sought-after development.

However, time is of the essence. Tower 3 is set to convert reservations to official sales on August 17, 2025. This date marks a critical point where units will be formally allocated.


Why a Priority Number is Your Golden Ticket

Here's where strategic planning comes into play: you still have a crucial window to secure a priority number before August 17, 2025. This isn't just a formality; it's a significant advantage that can make all the difference in getting the unit you truly desire.

Here’s why a priority number is so important:

  1. First Pick of the Best Units: Imagine having your pick of the most coveted units – those with the best views, ideal layouts, or most strategic locations. With a priority number, you gain the exclusive privilege to select your desired property before the general public. This means you won't have to contend with a mad rush or settle for what's left.

  2. Exclusive Access: Holders of priority numbers often get early access to floor plans, pricing, and specific unit details, allowing you to make an informed decision without pressure.

  3. Peace of Mind: Knowing you have a priority spot eliminates much of the stress and uncertainty associated with popular property launches. You've secured your place in line for a highly desirable property.


More Than Just a Condo: A Lifestyle Await

Even with the focus on Tower 3, the core appeal of Costa Mira Beachtown remains unchanged. This community is designed to deliver a resort-inspired lifestyle every single day. Future residents will enjoy:

  • Pristine Beach Access: Step directly from your home onto Mactan's beautiful sands.

  • Luxurious Amenities: Multiple swimming pools, a modern fitness gym, a vibrant clubhouse, and lush open spaces.

  • Strategic Location: Enjoy the tranquility of island life while remaining conveniently close to the Mactan-Cebu International Airport, CCLEX, and other essential city conveniences.

  • Thoughtful Design: Each unit and common area is crafted to maximize comfort, views, and the overall living experience.

Don't let this final opportunity slip away. If coastal living, investment potential, and a premium lifestyle are on your radar, now is the time to reach out and secure your priority number for Tower 3 at Costa Mira Beachtown. Your dream home in paradise is closer than you think!
