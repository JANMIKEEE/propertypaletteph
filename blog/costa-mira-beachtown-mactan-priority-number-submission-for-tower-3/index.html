Costa Mira Beachtown Mactan Priority Number Submission for Tower 3

Sep 3, 2025

Secure Your Slice of Paradise: Priority Number Submission for Tower 3 Opens September 10th!

The moment you've been waiting for is almost here! Following the rapid sell-out of Towers 1 and 2, Cebu Landmasters, Inc. is excited to announce that we are now preparing for the highly anticipated launch of Costa Mira Beachtown Mactan Tower 3.

Beginning on September 10, 2025, we will be accepting submissions to secure your Priority Number (PN). This is your exclusive chance to be among the first to choose and reserve the best units in the newest tower of this integrated beachfront community. With the incredible demand experienced in the previous towers, a Priority Number is your key to owning a piece of this tropical haven.

We strongly encourage everyone to prepare their requirements in advance of the said date. We are anticipating a large volume of submissions and having your documents ready will ensure a smooth and prompt process for securing your Priority Number.

What is a Priority Number and Why is it Important?
A Priority Number secures your place in line to select a unit when the official launch day arrives. Only those with a Priority Number will have the opportunity to choose and reserve a unit, giving you a distinct advantage and a higher chance of getting your preferred unit with the best views.


Property Review: Why Costa Mira Beachtown is a Prime Investment

Costa Mira Beachtown Mactan isn't just a condominium; it's a lifestyle destination and a strategic investment. Located in an idyllic and intimate spot on the southern coast of Mactan, the development is surrounded by lush tropical greenery and features unique nature-inspired architecture.

Key Highlights:

  • Integrated Beach Resort Community: The property seamlessly blends the comforts of a modern home with the allure of a tropical resort.

  • Strategic and Accessible Location:

    • 350 meters from Cebu Beach Club

    • 1 km from Subabasbas Town Hall

    • 2.5 km from Plantation Bay Resort & Spa

    • Just a 6-minute drive to the Cebu-Cordova Link Expressway (CCLEX)

    • 15 minutes to Mactan-Cebu International Airport

This prime location makes it an ideal spot for both a permanent residence and a vacation home, attracting a strong market of both local and international buyers and renters.


Potential Capital Appreciation

Investing in a pre-selling unit at Costa Mira Beachtown Mactan offers significant potential for capital appreciation. Here’s why this property is a smart financial choice:

  • Proven Demand: Towers 1 and 2 sold out at an impressive pace, a clear indicator of the strong market demand for this project.

  • Limited Supply: Beachfront condos in prime locations like Mactan Island are a rare commodity. This scarcity ensures long-term value appreciation.

  • Thriving Tourism Hub: Cebu's status as a top tourist destination, both locally and internationally, guarantees a steady demand for vacation rentals. This provides an excellent opportunity for passive income, which in turn drives property values up.

  • Early Buyer Advantage: Purchasing during the pre-selling phase allows you to buy at the lowest possible price, maximizing your potential returns as the project develops and prices increase.


Existing Improvements and Future-Proofing Your Investment

The Costa Mira Beachtown community is designed to provide a holistic and enriching living experience. The development already boasts a wide array of existing and planned amenities that add value to your investment:

Tower Features:

  • Adult & Kiddie Pool

  • Pool Deck

  • Function Rooms

  • Fitness Gym

  • Cabanas

  • Tropical Garden

  • Function Lawn

Community Features:

  • Retail Building

  • Al Fresco Dining

  • Tennis Court

  • Pool Bar

  • Active Park

  • Grand Event/Stepped Lawn

These world-class amenities not only enhance the living experience for residents but also contribute to the overall appeal and value of the property in the competitive Mactan real estate market.

Don't miss out on this golden opportunity to own your piece of Mactan's paradise. Prepare your requirements and get ready to secure your Priority Number on September 10, 2025!
