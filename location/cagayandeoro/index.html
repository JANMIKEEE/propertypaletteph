Why Invest in Cagayan de Oro City?

  1. Improved Infrastructure – Projects like the CDO Coastal Road and Laguindingan Airport expansion boost accessibility and property values.

  2. Rising Residential Demand – Increasing need for condominiums, subdivisions, and mixed-use developments near business hubs.

  3. Thriving Commercial Sector – Business parks, malls, and offices drive property appreciation and rental demand.

  4. Affordable Properties with High ROI – Lower prices than Metro Manila and Cebu, with strong rental yields and capital growth.

  5. Sustainable Development – Eco-friendly and disaster-resilient projects enhance livability and long-term value.
