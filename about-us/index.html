Our story

Welcome to Property Palette – your trusted partner in finding the perfect home or investment property.

Powered by Filipino Homes, the country’s largest real estate solutions portal, we give you access to a one-stop shop for buyers and sellers—both online and offline. From property listings and market insights to expert assessments and events, we provide everything you need to make smart real estate decisions.

Proudly part of Team Gforce under the guidance of George Ryan Sarmago (Licensed Real Estate Broker, PRC Lic. 0033596, DHSUD: CVRFO-A-01/19-3843), we bring professional expertise and genuine care to every transaction.

As members of the respected Filipino Homes / Leuterio Realty network, led by multi-awarded visionary realtor Anthony Leuterio, we connect you to unparalleled industry experience, diverse property options, and a strong commitment to excellence.

Whether you’re buying, selling, or investing, Property Palette is here to make your real estate journey seamless, rewarding, and truly exceptional.
