Frequently asked questions

What types of properties do you offer?

We offer a wide range of properties including residential communities, lifestyle developments, mixed-use projects, and premium estates across key locations in the Philippines.

How can I schedule a site visit?

You can book a site visit through our website’s contact form or by reaching out to us via Messenger or WhatsApp. We’ll be happy to arrange a convenient time and assist you during your visit.

What financing options are available?

We work with major banks and financial institutions to provide flexible payment terms and financing options. Pag-IBIG, in-house financing, and bank financing are commonly available for qualified buyers.

Can I buy a property even if I’m based overseas?

Yes, we assist both local and international buyers, including OFWs. Our team can coordinate online presentations, virtual tours, and document processing to make it easy for overseas clients.

Can foreigners buy property in the Philippines?

Foreigners may own condominium units but not land. However, they can invest through long-term leases or by purchasing property under certain legal structures.

What are the advantages of buying early in a project?

Buying early usually means lower prices, more flexible payment terms, and the best choice of units compared to later phases.
